  • Goddard Riverside Community Center

    Goddard Riverside Community Center is one of New York City's leading human service organizations and is based in the Upper West Side. Goddard's mission is to "meet people's basic needs -- food, shelter, education -- and to bring them together for mutual aid, social action, and to celebrate our richness as a society."

    Since 2007, each Thanksgiving Day and Christmas Day, my family and I (and a couple hundred other volunteers) have worked with Goddard to host and serve a free, warm, home-cooked, sit-down holiday dinner. Volunteers help to prepare and serve over 3000 meals that are both given to visitors and delivered to home-bound locals who cannot attend the dinner. According to Goddard, "Guests include people in need, homeless adults, elderly individuals and families. The dinners have been a neighborhood tradition for more than 40 years."
